Quality and compliance are fundamental to every project executed by Valstom Energy. We follow a structured quality approach to ensure that all supplied materials and integrated packages meet applicable standards, specifications, and customer expectations.

Material Certification

Material Test Certificates supplied in accordance with EN 10204:

  • 2.1 / 2.2 / 3.1 / 3.2, as specified by project and client requirements.
  • Full traceability maintained from manufacturer to final delivery.

Standards & Compliance

  • Products and documentation aligned with applicable API, ASTM, ASME, ISO, and relevant international standards.
  • Compliance ensured against project specifications, datasheets, and contractual requirements.

Inspection & Testing

  • Internal inspection and verification carried out prior to dispatch.
  • Functional and Hydrostatic Testing arranged where applicable.
  • Third-Party Inspection (TPI) can be arranged upon request through recognized agencies such as BV, TUV, DNV, Lloyd’s, or client-nominated inspectors.
  • Customer and inspector visits to our facilities can be coordinated when required.

Vendor Qualification

  • Manufacturers and suppliers are evaluated based on technical capability, quality systems, certifications, and past performance.
  • Continuous review to ensure consistent quality and reliable project execution.

Quality Assurance

  • Document control and review to ensure accuracy, completeness, and traceability.
  • Inspection and test records maintained as part of project documentation.
  • Focus on delivering compliant, ready-for-installation equipment to minimize site risk and delays.

Valstom Energy is committed to delivering reliable, compliant, and quality-assured solutions that support safe operations, long-term performance, and customer confidence.
