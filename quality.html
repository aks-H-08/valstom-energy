Committed to Quality

Quality and compliance are fundamental to every project executed by Valstom Energy. We follow a structured quality approach to ensure that all supplied materials and integrated packages meet applicable standards, project specifications, and customer expectations.

Our quality processes are embedded across procurement, inspection, testing, documentation, and delivery to ensure reliable, ready-for-installation solutions.

Material Certification

  • Material Test Certificates (MTCs) as per EN 10204 – 2.1 / 2.2 / 3.1 / 3.2
  • Certification aligned with project and client requirements
  • Full material traceability from manufacturer to final delivery

Standards & Compliance

  • Products aligned with API, ASTM, ASME, ISO, and relevant standards
  • Documentation reviewed against project datasheets and specifications
  • Compliance ensured with contractual and regulatory requirements

Inspection & Testing

  • Internal inspection and verification prior to dispatch
  • Functional and hydrostatic testing arranged where applicable
  • Third-Party Inspection (TPI) via BV, TÜV, DNV, Lloyd’s or client-nominated agencies
  • Customer and inspector visits coordinated when required

Vendor Qualification

  • Supplier evaluation based on technical capability and certifications
  • Assessment of quality systems and past project performance
  • Continuous review to ensure reliable execution and consistency

Quality Assurance & Documentation

  • Controlled document review for accuracy and traceability
  • Inspection and test records maintained within project dossiers
  • Focus on compliant, ready-for-installation equipment delivery

Commitment to Quality

Valstom Energy is committed to delivering reliable, compliant, and quality-assured solutions that support safe operations, long-term performance, and customer confidence across all served markets.
