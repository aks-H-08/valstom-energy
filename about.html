01.

Who We Are

Valstom delivers integrated Project Packages across Valves, Actuators, Pipes, Steel Plates, Wellhead Equipment, Drilling Components, and related Electrical Items, supporting critical projects in the oil & gas, petrochemical, refining, power, and water sectors.

Led by experienced industry professionals and supported by a strong global supplier and manufacturer network, we provide complete, compliant, and documentation-ready packages aligned with project requirements.

Our focus on technical clarity, accurate documentation, reliable coordination, and fast delivery—including urgent and shutdown support backed by available stock—ensures dependable performance and operational continuity for our customers.

02.

Vision

To leverage our expertise in complex energy projects and become a trusted, forward-thinking materials partner for the global energy sector—delivering value, reliability, and innovation while contributing responsibly to a more sustainable and resilient energy future.

03.

Mission

To be the energy industry’s dependable partner for valves, piping, structural steel, and specialized equipment— delivering strong project coordination, reliable supply chain support, and timely, compliant solutions while upholding safe, ethical, and sustainable business practices.

04.

Core Values

Our core values guide every decision we make, defining who we are and how we operate. They reflect our commitment to ethical conduct, sustainable practices, and the well-being of our people, helping us build long-term trust with customers, partners, and communities.
