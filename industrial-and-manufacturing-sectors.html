Industrial & Manufacturing Sectors

Valstom Energy supports industrial and manufacturing sectors by supplying specification-driven equipment packages designed for demanding operating conditions, continuous processes, and plant reliability.

Our focus is on flow control, pressure management, piping, pumping, and associated systems delivered with full documentation, compliance, and execution support.

Mining & Mineral Processing

Valstom Energy supplies equipment for mining and mineral processing facilities operating under abrasive, corrosive, and high-wear conditions. Our solutions support ore handling, slurry transport, beneficiation, and material processing systems where durability and reliability are critical.

Equipment is selected based on erosion resistance, pressure class, and service severity in accordance with project specifications.

  • Severe-service and slurry valves
  • Knife gate and isolation valves
  • Abrasion-resistant piping materials
  • Actuated valve systems for process control
  • Pressure control and isolation equipment
  • Project and maintenance support

Metals & Steel Industries

Valstom Energy supports steel plants and metal processing facilities with equipment packages designed for high-temperature operations, heavy-duty service, and continuous production environments.

Our supply scope aligns with plant utility systems, process lines, and auxiliary services supporting steelmaking and metal processing operations.

  • Isolation and control valves
  • High-temperature and severe-service valves
  • Piping materials, plates, and bars
  • Pressure control equipment
  • Actuation and automation packages
  • Maintenance and shutdown support

Cement Plants

Valstom Energy supplies equipment packages for cement manufacturing facilities operating under dusty, abrasive, and high-temperature conditions. Our solutions support raw material handling, kiln systems, grinding circuits, and utility services within cement plants.

Equipment is selected to ensure reliability, durability, and ease of maintenance.

  • Isolation and shut-off valves
  • Knife gate and specialty valves for bulk handling
  • Abrasion-resistant piping and fittings
  • Pressure control and utility valves
  • Actuated valve packages
  • Plant maintenance and shutdown support
