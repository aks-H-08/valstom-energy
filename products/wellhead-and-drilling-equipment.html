Wellhead and Drilling Equipment

Wellhead & Drilling Equipment

Valstom Energy supplies wellhead and drilling equipment for upstream Oil & Gas applications, supporting exploration, production, and well intervention activities.

Equipment is supplied to meet demanding pressure, temperature, and service conditions typical of upstream environments, with full material traceability, certification, and compliance to project, operator, and regulatory requirements.

Wellhead Equipment

Wellhead equipment is supplied to provide pressure containment, flow control, and mechanical support at the well interface throughout drilling, completion, and production phases.

Components are selected based on working pressure, temperature rating, service media, and well configuration, ensuring compatibility with casing and tubing programs and compliance with applicable well design and safety standards.

Product Types

  • Casing heads and hangers
  • Tubing heads and hangers
  • Christmas tree components
  • Valves and accessories
  • Studs and sealing components

Design Standards & Certification

Applicable Standards

  • API 6A – Wellhead and Christmas Tree Equipment
  • NACE MR0175 / ISO 15156 – Sour service materials
  • API 17D – Subsea and related components (where applicable)

Certification & Traceability

  • Material Test Certificates per EN 10204 3.1 / 3.2
  • Heat treatment and hardness records
  • Full material and manufacturing traceability

Drilling Equipment

Drilling equipment and accessories are supplied to support drilling operations, rig maintenance, and well control activities under demanding operating conditions.

Products are selected to withstand high mechanical loads, cyclic stresses, and abrasive environments, and are supplied in accordance with applicable API standards and project-specific technical requirements.

Product Types

  • Drilling tools
  • Rig spare parts
  • Well control accessories
  • OCTG accessories

Standards & Verification

  • API 7-1 / API 7-2 – Rotary drill stem elements
  • API 16A – Drilling control equipment
  • Dimensional, thread, and material inspection
  • Hardness and mechanical property verification
