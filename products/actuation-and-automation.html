Actuation & Automation

Valstom Energy supplies valve actuation and automation solutions for isolation, control, and emergency shutdown applications across Oil & Gas and process facilities.

Actuation and automation packages are supplied as part of integrated project scopes, aligned with valve selection, control philosophy, and applicable international standards.

Actuator Types

Valve actuators are supplied to provide reliable mechanical operation of valves under defined process and environmental conditions. Actuator selection is based on torque requirements, operating philosophy, fail-safe criteria, and site conditions defined in project datasheets.

Actuator Options

  • Pneumatic actuators
  • Hydraulic actuators
  • Electric actuators
  • Gas-Over-Oil (GOO) actuators
  • Spring-return and double-acting configurations

Standards & Compatibility

  • Actuator sizing and interface per ISO 5211
  • Material compatibility with connected valves
  • Ambient and service temperature suitability
  • Sealing materials: NBR, FKM, EPDM

Automation & Accessories

Automation systems and accessories are supplied to enable safe, controlled, and monitored valve operation for on–off, modulating, and emergency shutdown functions.

Components are integrated in accordance with project control architecture, instrumentation requirements, and functional safety philosophy.

Automation Components

  • ESDV, SDV, MOV, and BDV packages
  • Solenoid valves
  • Positioners and limit switches
  • Air filter regulators

Standards & Certification

  • HART / Fieldbus compatible smart accessories
  • SIL-rated assemblies per IEC 61508 / IEC 61511
  • ATEX / IECEx for hazardous areas
  • IP65 / IP66 protection classes
  • Fail-safe and fire-safe configurations
