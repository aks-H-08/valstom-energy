Actuation & Automation

Valstom Energy supplies valve actuation and automation systems for isolation, control, and emergency shutdown applications across oil & gas, petrochemical, power, water, and industrial process facilities.

Actuators and automation assemblies are supplied as part of integrated valve packages or standalone project scopes, selected in accordance with valve torque requirements, operating conditions, safety considerations, and site requirements. All systems are supplied with OEM documentation, testing, and certification aligned with project specifications and applicable international standards.

Actuators

Valve actuators are supplied to provide reliable mechanical operation of valves under defined process and environmental conditions. Selection is based on required torque, fail-safe function, duty cycle, and service environment.

Actuator Types • Pneumatic actuators
• Hydraulic actuators
• Electric actuators
• Gas-over-oil actuators
• Spring-return and double-acting configurations
Technical Alignment • ISO 5211 mounting interface compatibility
• Sized based on valve torque calculations and service factors
• Materials compatible with valve metallurgy and operating environment
• Suitable for onshore and offshore installations
Standards & Certification • ATEX / IECEx for hazardous areas
• IEC 60079 compliant equipment, where applicable
• IP65 / IP66 / IP67 enclosure protection
• SIL-capable actuators in accordance with IEC 61508 / IEC 61511, where required
Testing & Documentation • Functional testing
• Torque verification
• Factory acceptance testing (FAT), where required
• OEM datasheets and test certificates

Automation Packages

Valve automation packages are supplied to enable remote and automated operation of actuated valves for isolation, shutdown, and process service. Assemblies are configured based on valve type, actuator configuration, operating conditions, and project specifications, and supplied as ready-to-install actuator-mounted systems.

Typical Applications • ESDV (Emergency Shutdown Valves)
• SDV (Shutdown Valves)
• MOV (Motor Operated Valves)
• BDV (Blowdown Valves)
• On–off and modulating valve service
Assembly Scope Automation assemblies are supplied with actuator-mounted control components and monitoring devices suitable for site operating conditions and hazardous area classification.
Communication & Functional Capability • Communication interfaces such as Modbus or hardwired signals, where specified
• Position feedback and status indication
• Partial stroke testing (PST) capability for shutdown valves, where required
• Suitable for local and remote operation in accordance with project requirements
Compliance All automation packages are supplied in accordance with actuator manufacturer standards, project specifications, and applicable hazardous area and electrical certification requirements, with functional testing and documentation provided prior to delivery.

Controls & Accessories

Control and accessory components are supplied to enable monitoring, feedback, and reliable operation of actuated valves in field conditions.

Typical Scope • Air filter regulators (AFR) with gauges
• Solenoid valves
• Valve positioners (smart or conventional)
• Limit switch boxes and position feedback units
• Tubing, fittings, and manifolds
• Local control stations and junction boxes
• Weatherproof or explosion-proof enclosures
• Manual override systems
• Gearboxes and handwheel overrides
• Mounting brackets and couplings
Selection Basis All accessories are selected based on actuator type, hazardous area classification, environmental conditions, and project specifications.

Standards & Compliance

Applicable Standards • ISO 5211 actuator mounting interface
• IEC / EN applicable electrical standards
• SIL requirements, where defined in project scope
• Hazardous area certification (ATEX / IECEx)
• Manufacturer testing and certification
Installation & Documentation Assemblies are supplied for reliable operation in both onshore and offshore installations and provided with full OEM documentation and test records.
