Pumps and Ancillary Equipment

Valstom Energy supplies industrial pumps for fluid handling, transfer, and process applications across Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power, Water, and Process Industries. Pumps are supplied on a project basis, selected in accordance with process conditions, duty requirements, and applicable industry standards, and delivered with full OEM documentation and certification.

Pump supply is aligned with project process datasheets, material compatibility requirements, and operational reliability expectations, ensuring equipment is compliant, traceable, and ready for installation within project timelines.

Pump Types

Pumps are supplied for continuous and intermittent service in process, utility, and chemical applications. Selection is based on required flow rate, differential head, fluid properties, operating temperature, pressure conditions, and service environment.

Product Scope • Centrifugal Pumps
• Process Pumps
• Chemical & Utility Pumps
• Horizontal and Vertical Configurations where specified

Technical Coverage – Pumps

Design Standards • API 610 – Centrifugal Pumps for Petroleum, Petrochemical, and Natural Gas Industries (where applicable)
• ISO 5199 / ISO 2858 – Industrial Pump Standards
• Manufacturer standards aligned with project specifications
Materials of Construction • Carbon Steel
• Stainless Steel
• Duplex & Super Duplex Stainless Steel
• Alloy Steel
• Special alloys as required by process fluid compatibility
Operating Range • Flow and head as defined by project process conditions
• Temperature and pressure limits in accordance with selected design standards and OEM data

Mechanical Seals, Seal Systems & Spares

Mechanical seals, seal support systems, and pump maintenance spares can be supplied as part of the pump package or as separate items in accordance with project and operational requirements.

Typical Scope • Mechanical seals (single or double)
• Seal support systems and seal pots
• Seal kits and replacement components
• Pump maintenance spares and wear parts
Standards Alignment Mechanical seals and related components are supplied in accordance with applicable standards such as API 682 where specified and aligned with pump service conditions and project specifications.

Testing & Inspection

Scope • Hydrostatic pressure testing
• Performance testing at OEM works
• Vibration and mechanical integrity checks (as applicable)
• Inspection and test reports supplied with documentation
Execution Inspection and testing activities are aligned with project specifications and coordinated with manufacturers and third-party inspectors where required.

Documentation

Deliverables • OEM data sheets and performance curves
• Test certificates and inspection reports
• Material Test Certificates (MTC)
• Compliance documentation aligned with project requirements
Review Documentation is reviewed prior to dispatch to ensure completeness, traceability, and alignment with project specifications.
