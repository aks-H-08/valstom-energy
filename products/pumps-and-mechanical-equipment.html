Pumps & Ancillary Equipment

Pumps

Valstom Energy supplies industrial pumps for fluid handling, transfer, and process applications across Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power, Water, and Process Industries.

Pumps are supplied on a project basis, selected in accordance with process conditions, duty requirements, and applicable industry standards, and delivered with full OEM documentation and certification.

Pump supply is aligned with project process datasheets, material compatibility requirements, and operational reliability expectations.

Pump Types & Technical Coverage

Pumps are supplied for continuous and intermittent service in process, utility, and chemical applications. Selection is based on required flow rate, differential head, fluid properties, operating temperature, pressure conditions, and service environment.

Product Scope

  • Centrifugal pumps
  • Process pumps
  • Chemical and utility pumps

Design Standards

  • API 610 – Centrifugal pumps (where applicable)
  • ISO 5199 / ISO 2858 – Industrial pump standards
  • Manufacturer standards aligned with project specifications

Materials of Construction

  • Carbon steel
  • Stainless steel
  • Duplex & Super Duplex stainless steel
  • Alloy steel and special alloys as required

Operating & Mechanical Design

  • Flow and head per project process conditions
  • Temperature and pressure limits per design standard
  • Mechanical seals per API 682 (where specified)

Testing, Inspection & Documentation

  • Hydrostatic and performance testing at OEM works
  • Vibration and mechanical integrity checks
  • OEM datasheets, curves, and test certificates
  • Inspection reports and Material Test Certificates
