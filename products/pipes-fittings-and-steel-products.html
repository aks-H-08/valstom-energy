Pipes, Fittings, and Steel Products

Pipes, Fittings & Steel Products

Valstom Energy supplies piping materials and steel products for EPC and industrial projects supporting upstream, midstream, downstream, and general industrial applications.

Materials are supplied on a project basis with full traceability, certification, and documentation in line with applicable international standards and project specifications.

Pipes

Pipes are supplied for fluid transportation in process, utility, and pipeline services. Selection is based on design pressure, temperature, corrosion allowance, and service conditions defined by applicable codes and project requirements.

Material Grades

  • Carbon Steel: ASTM A106 Gr. B/C, ASTM A53 Gr. B
  • Alloy Steel: ASTM A335 P1, P5, P9, P11, P22
  • Stainless Steel: ASTM A312 TP304/304L, TP316/316L
  • Duplex / Super Duplex: ASTM A789 / A790 S31803 / S32205
  • Chrome Moly: ASTM A335 P91 (project dependent)

Design & Testing

  • ASME B31.3 / ASME B31.1 / API 5L
  • Hydrostatic testing per ASME / API
  • NDT: UT, RT, PMI, MPI as required
  • EN 10204 3.1 / 3.2 Material Test Certificates

Fittings & Flanges

Fittings and flanges are supplied to support piping layout, directional changes, pressure containment, and mechanical connections. Products are selected based on piping class, pressure rating, dimensional standards, and material compatibility.

Material Grades

  • Carbon Steel: ASTM A234 WPB / WPL6
  • Alloy Steel: ASTM A234 WP11 / WP22 / WP91
  • Stainless Steel: ASTM A403 WP304 / WP316
  • Forged fittings: ASTM A105

Standards & Inspection

  • ASME B16.9 / B16.11 / B16.5
  • MSS SP-43 / SP-75 (where applicable)
  • Visual, dimensional, and NDT inspection

Steel Plates & Bars

Steel plates and bars are supplied for pressure-containing equipment, structural fabrication, and industrial manufacturing applications. Material selection is based on mechanical properties, service conditions, and applicable standards.

Material Grades

  • Carbon Steel: ASTM A516 Gr. 60 / 70
  • Alloy Steel: ASTM A387 Gr. 11 / 22
  • Stainless Steel: ASTM A240 TP304 / 316
  • Duplex / Super Duplex: ASTM A240 S31803 / S32205
  • Abrasion / corrosion resistant plates

Certification & Traceability

  • ASTM / ASME / EN / DIN compliance
  • EN 10204 3.1 / 3.2 certification
  • Chemical & mechanical property verification
