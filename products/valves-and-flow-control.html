Valves & Flow Control

Valstom Energy supplies industrial valves and flow control equipment for isolation, regulation, pressure control, and safety functions across Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power, Water, and Process Industries.

Valves are supplied on a project basis, aligned with EPC specifications, client requirements, and applicable international standards to ensure operational integrity and compliance.

Standard Valve Portfolio

The Standard Valve Portfolio covers valves primarily used for isolation and on–off duties in process, utility, and pipeline services. These valves are selected based on pressure class, material compatibility, and service conditions defined by the project.

Product Scope

  • Ball, Gate, Globe, and Check Valves
  • Butterfly, Plug, Knife Gate Valves
  • Angle Globe Valves and Strainers

Typical Applications

  • Isolation and on–off service
  • Utility and process lines
  • Non-modulating flow applications

Technical Coverage

  • API 6D, API 600 / 602 / 603, ISO 17292
  • ASME B16 Series, BS / DIN / EN Standards
  • Wide pressure class, size range, and materials
  • Hydrostatic, seat testing, PMI, NDT, and TPI

Specialized Valve Portfolio

The Specialized Valve Portfolio addresses severe, critical, and special service applications where operating conditions exceed conventional limits. These valves are supplied strictly in accordance with project-specific process and mechanical requirements.

Product Scope

  • DBB and Dual Expanding DBB Valves
  • Slab, Through Conduit & Expansion Gate Valves
  • Choke, Subsea, Cryogenic & Hydrogen Valves
  • Lined Valves (PTFE / PFA)

Technical Coverage

  • API 6D / API 6A, API 607 / 6FA (Fire Safe)
  • ISO 15848 (Fugitive Emissions)
  • NACE, cryogenic, and severe service compliance
  • Fire-safe, emission, and low-temperature testing

Control Valves & Pressure Control

Control and pressure control valves are supplied for applications requiring continuous flow regulation, pressure management, and system protection as part of process control loops.

Product Scope

  • Globe, Ball, and Butterfly Control Valves
  • Pressure Safety Valves (PSV)
  • Pressure Relief and Reducing Valves

Standards & Testing

  • IEC 60534, API 520 / 526 / 527
  • Functional, performance, and set-pressure testing
  • Noise and performance validation where specified

High Integrity Pressure Protection Systems (HIPPS)

High Integrity Pressure Protection Systems (HIPPS) are applied in Oil & Gas and process facilities to provide automatic overpressure protection by isolating the pressure source before the design limits of downstream equipment are exceeded.

HIPPS are implemented where conventional relief systems are not feasible or where reduction of flare loads is required. Supplied as part of HIPPS assemblies in accordance with project SIS requirements.

Typical System Scope

  • Shutdown valves (final elements)
  • Valve actuation systems
  • Pressure sensing instruments
  • Logic solvers and control interfaces

Design & Compliance

  • IEC 61508 / IEC 61511
  • SIL-rated components
  • Fail-safe and fire-safe philosophy
