Valves & Flow Control

Valstom Energy supplies industrial valves and flow control equipment for isolation, regulation, pressure control, and safety functions across Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power, Water, and Process Industries. Valves are supplied on a project basis, aligned with EPC specifications, client requirements, and applicable international standards.

Standard Valve Portfolio

The Standard Valve Portfolio covers valves used primarily for isolation and on–off duties in process, utility, and pipeline services. These valves are typically applied where flow control is not required and are selected based on pressure class, material compatibility, and service conditions defined by the project.

• Utility and process lines
• Non-modulating applications

Control & Safety Valves

Control and Safety Valves are supplied for applications requiring continuous flow regulation, pressure management, and system protection. These valves form an integral part of process control loops and overpressure protection systems, selected based on process conditions, control requirements, and applicable international standards.

Product Scope • Globe Control Valves
• Ball Control Valves
• Butterfly Control Valves
• Pressure Safety Valves (PSV)
• Pressure Relief Valves (PRV)
• Pressure Reducing Valves
Design Standards • IEC 60534 (Control Valves)
• API 520 / 526 / 527 (Pressure Relief & Safety Valves)
• ASME interface and dimensional standards, where applicable
Testing & Verification • Functional and performance testing
• Set pressure and blowdown testing
• Noise and performance validation, where specified

High Integrity Pressure Protection Systems (HIPPS)

High Integrity Pressure Protection Systems (HIPPS) are applied in Oil & Gas and process facilities to provide automatic overpressure protection by isolating the pressure source before the design limits of downstream equipment are exceeded.

HIPPS are implemented where conventional relief systems are not feasible or where reduction of flare loads is required. Supplied as part of HIPPS assemblies in accordance with project SIS requirements.

Typical System Scope • Shutdown valves (final elements)
• Valve actuation systems
• Pressure sensing instruments
• Logic solver interface components (where within supply scope)
• Associated accessories and interconnections
Design & Compliance • IEC 61508 / IEC 61511
• SIL-rated components
• Fail-safe and Fire-safe
