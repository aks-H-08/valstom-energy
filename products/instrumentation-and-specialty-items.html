Instrumentation & Specialty Items

Valstom Energy supplies industrial instrumentation and specialty engineered items for measurement, monitoring, and system protection across Oil & Gas and process industries.

These products support safe plant operation, accurate process control, and long-term system integrity, and are supplied in accordance with project specifications, applicable standards, and site operating conditions.

Instrumentation

Instrumentation is supplied for accurate measurement and monitoring of pressure, temperature, level, and flow parameters in process and utility services. Instrument selection is based on accuracy, response time, operating pressure and temperature, hazardous area classification, and compatibility with plant control and safety systems.

Product Types

  • Pressure gauges and transmitters
  • Temperature instruments (RTDs, thermocouples)
  • Level and flow instruments
  • Instrumentation valves and manifolds

Standards & Calibration

  • IEC / ISO instrumentation standards
  • ATEX / IECEx compliance for hazardous areas
  • Calibration and test certificates supplied

Gaskets & Fasteners

Gaskets and fasteners are supplied to ensure pressure-tight sealing and mechanically secure connections across piping, valves, and equipment assemblies. Materials are selected to maintain joint integrity under operating and cyclic loading conditions.

Product Types

  • Spiral wound and RTJ gaskets
  • Soft gaskets (PTFE, graphite, CAF)
  • Stud bolts, nuts, and fasteners

Applicable Standards

  • ASME B16.20 for gaskets
  • ASTM fastener standards (A193, A194, etc.)

Specialty Engineered Items

Specialty engineered items are supplied to support sampling, chemical injection, corrosion monitoring, expansion management, and flow measurement within process systems.

Components are selected based on process conditions, material compatibility, pressure and temperature limits, and installation requirements to ensure reliable operation and accurate system performance.

Product Types

  • Sampling systems
  • Injection quills and fixed probes
  • Corrosion coupons and holders
  • Expansion bellows
  • Orifice plates and flow elements

Standards & Compliance

  • ASME / ANSI standards where applicable
  • Test certificates with full material traceability
