Instrumentation & Specialty Items

Valstom Energy supplies industrial instrumentation and specialty engineered items for measurement, monitoring, and system protection across Oil & Gas and process industries. These products support safe plant operation, accurate process control, and long-term system integrity, and are supplied in accordance with project specifications, applicable standards, and site operating conditions.

Instrumentation

Instrumentation is supplied for accurate measurement and monitoring of pressure, temperature, level, and flow parameters in process and utility services. Instrument selection is based on required accuracy, response time, operating pressure and temperature, hazardous area classification, and compatibility with plant control and safety systems.

Product Types • Pressure Gauges & Transmitters
• Temperature Instruments (RTDs, Thermocouples)
• Level & Flow Instruments
• Instrumentation Valves & Manifolds
Standards & Calibration • IEC / ISO instrument standards
• ATEX / IECEx for hazardous areas
• Calibration and test certificates supplied

Gaskets & Fasteners

Gaskets and fasteners are supplied to ensure pressure-tight sealing and mechanically secure connections across piping, valves, and equipment assemblies. Materials are selected based on pressure class, temperature range, service media, and flange facing requirements to maintain joint integrity under operating and cyclic loading conditions.

Product Types • Spiral Wound & RTJ Gaskets
• Soft Gaskets (PTFE, Graphite, CAF)
• Stud Bolts, Nuts & Fasteners
Standards • ASME B16.20 for gaskets
• ASTM fastener standards (A193, A194, etc.)

Specialty Engineered Items

Specialty engineered items are supplied to support sampling, chemical injection, corrosion monitoring, expansion management, and flow measurement within process systems. These components are selected based on process conditions, material compatibility, pressure and temperature limits, and installation requirements, ensuring reliable operation and accurate system performance.

Product Types • Sampling Systems
• Injection Quills
• Fixed Probes
• Corrosion Coupons & Holders
• Expansion Bellows
• Orifice Plates & Flow Elements
Standards & Compliance • ASME / ANSI standards where applicable
• Test certificates supplied with full material traceability
