Energy & Process Industries

Valstom Energy supports core energy and process sectors by supplying integrated equipment packages engineered to meet sector-specific operating conditions, international standards, and project execution requirements.

Our focus is on critical flow control, pressure management, piping, pumping, and associated systems delivered with full documentation, compliance, and execution reliability.

Upstream Oil & Gas

Valstom Energy supports upstream Oil & Gas operations covering exploration, drilling, completion, production, and well intervention activities in high-pressure, high-temperature, sour service, and safety-critical environments.

We support both onshore and offshore developments, including subsea applications, through project-specific equipment packages aligned with operator standards, EPC specifications, and applicable API codes.

  • Valves and flow control equipment for critical upstream duties
  • Subsea valves and offshore components
  • Wellhead and drilling equipment (API 6A)
  • Actuation, automation, and ESD systems
  • HIPPS components for overpressure protection
  • Piping materials and specialty items
  • Shutdown, urgent, and time-critical support

Midstream

Valstom Energy supplies equipment packages for midstream infrastructure supporting the transportation, processing, and storage of hydrocarbons.

Equipment selection is based on pressure class, flow requirements, and safety philosophy defined by the project and applicable standards.

  • Pipeline, isolation, and block valves
  • Pressure control and safety valves
  • Actuated valve packages for automated operation
  • HIPPS components for pipeline protection
  • Pipes, fittings, and flanges
  • Project and maintenance documentation support

Downstream

Valstom Energy supports downstream facilities including refineries and processing plants with specification-driven equipment packages for continuous operation and safety-critical services.

  • Process, control, and severe-service valves
  • Pressure safety and relief valves
  • Actuated shutdown and control systems
  • HIPPS components where required
  • Piping materials and specialty items
  • Integrated testing, inspection, and documentation

Petrochemical

Valstom Energy supplies equipment packages for petrochemical facilities handling aggressive media, elevated temperatures, and continuous processing operations.

  • Process and isolation valves
  • Control and pressure-regulating valves
  • Actuated valve systems
  • Lined and specialty material valves
  • Pipes, steel plates, and bars
  • Compliance documentation and inspection coordination

Power Generation

Valstom Energy supports conventional and industrial power generation facilities with equipment packages designed for reliability, pressure control, and operational continuity.

  • Actuation and automation solutions
  • Maintenance and shutdown support

LNG & Cryogenic Facilities

Valstom Energy supplies materials and flow control solutions for LNG and cryogenic applications where low-temperature performance and leak-tight integrity are critical.

  • Cryogenic-grade materials
  • Specialty fittings and accessories
  • Project-specific compliance support
